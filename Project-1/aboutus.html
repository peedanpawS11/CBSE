  The Group

The DelGamma Capital, founded in 1934, operates 31 hotels, Nile Cruisers and a Motor Vessel in the backwaters of Kerala. The Group has presence in six countries under the luxury 'IHG' and five-star 'DelGamma' brand. The Group is also engaged in flight catering, airport restaurants, travel and tour services, car rentals, project management and corporate air charters.

DG Capital Hotels & Resorts

DG Capital Hotels & Resorts is synonymous the world over with providing the right blend of service, luxury and quiet efficiency. Internationally acclaimed for all-round excellence and unparalleled levels of service, DG Capital Hotels & Resorts have received innumerable awards and accolades. A distinctive feature of The Group's hotels is their highly motivated and well trained staff who provide exceptionally attentive, personalised and warm service. The Group's luxury hotels have established a reputation for redefining the paradigm of luxury and excellence in service amongst leisure hotels around the world. IHG hotels are five-star hotels that have established a reputation for excellence and are acknowledged for offering quality and value. These hotels combine state of the art facilities with dependable service in a caring environment, presenting the ideal choice for business and leisure travellers.
